# B2B (future)

Agentsyx is evolving the **customer-facing** experience toward **organization-level** control for business teams.

## Direction (not yet complete)

Planned themes include:

- **Team admin** — A customer-side role that administers **their** org (not the operator’s Creator org).
- **User roles** — Who can use which apps, agents, or spend credits within that customer org.
- **Org-wide defaults** — Shared **workflows**, **agents**, and **apps** for the whole team instead of only per-user setup.

When these ship, this help site will be expanded. Until then, treat this page as **roadmap context** only.
