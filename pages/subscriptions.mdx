# Subscriptions

**Subscription plans** (prices, tiers, trials, what each plan includes) are **defined by each app’s creator** in **[Agentsyx Creator](https://creator.agentsyx.com)** for their project and deployment. **agentsyx.com** does not invent plans — it shows **what they set up** and lets **you** **pick**, **upgrade**, or **manage** those plans.

You’ll see and act on subscriptions in several places. Use this page to know **where to look**.

## Sidebar: My Subscriptions / My Subscription

The sidebar entry (label may be **plural or singular**) opens a **dedicated view** of your **active subscriptions** and **plan management** for apps you use through Agentsyx. Use it for questions like “Where do I upgrade?” or “Which plan am I on?”

## While you use the Agent workspace

You do **not** have to open **My Subscriptions** first. Plan prompts can appear **inside** the **[Agent workspace](/agent)** in two places — both still use **only** plans **the creator** set up in **Creator** for those apps.

### The tab labeled “Creator” (on agentsyx.com)

This is **not** the operator console at **creator.agentsyx.com**. On the **customer** site, **Creator** is the **name of a tab** next to **App**, **Directory**, and so on. See **[Creator (tab)](/agent/creator-tab)**.

There you **browse and open discoverable apps** for the **creator’s project** you’re in. When an app needs a plan, Agentsyx walks you through **subscribe**, **upgrade**, or similar — always based on **that creator’s** plans and pricing.

### The “App” tab (embedded panel)

This tab shows the **embedded app** for whichever app is in focus. If you **don’t** have the entitlement their rules require, you may see **subscription required**, **pick a plan**, or **start a free trial** (when they offer a **zero-price** plan). After entitlement is satisfied, the panel runs normally. See **[App](/agent/app-panel)**.

## Relationship to usage and limits

**Credits** and limits show in **[Usage](/usage)**. If a tool **stops working**, check both **subscription** and **usage** before assuming a problem with the chat app you’re using.
