# Creator (tab on agentsyx.com)

On the **customer** site, **Creator** is the **name of a tab** inside the Agent workspace. It is **not** the same as **[Agentsyx Creator](https://creator.agentsyx.com)** — the **operator console** where creators configure connectors, plans, and billing.

## What this tab is for

Use **Creator** to **browse discoverable apps** for the **project** you’re currently in and to **open** them. When a creator marks apps as discoverable, they show up here for eligible users.

## Opening an app

- Select an app and use **Open** (or equivalent) to navigate into that app’s experience. The URL updates so you can **bookmark** or **share** the path.
- After opening, you may land in the **[App](/agent/app-panel)** tab or the flow the creator designed.

## Trials and plans

The **first time** you use apps from a **creator** you haven’t subscribed with before, Agentsyx **creates a trial subscription automatically** (using **that creator’s** trial setup in Agentsyx). You should **not** be pushed straight to **paid checkout** for that initial trial. Later, **upgrades** or **renewals** follow **only** what the **creator** configured.

More detail: **[Subscriptions](/subscriptions)**.

## After you’re connected

Once you’re entitled to an app (including while on **trial**), it can appear under **Available Apps** in **[Config](/agent/config)** so you can **connect** it to your agent and use its **tools** in **[Agent (chat)](/agent/chat)**.
