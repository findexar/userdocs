# Agent workspace

The **Agent workspace** is usually the **home** view after you open a **creator’s app or page** on **[agentsyx.com](https://www.agentsyx.com)**. It combines **chat**, **how your agent is set up**, **connected apps**, **embedded app panels**, and **browsing other creators’ apps**.

## Tabs

On **desktop**, tabs are split (some on the left, some on the right). On **small screens** they appear in **one scrollable row**.

| Tab | What it’s for | Help |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Agent** | Chat with your agent; tools from **connected** apps run from here. | **[Agent (chat)](/agent/chat)** |
| **Config** | Pick or create an agent, edit **name / description / system message**, connect apps, inspect **tools / resources / prompts**. | **[Config](/agent/config)** |
| **App** | Full **embedded app** (widgets, panels) for the app in focus—often opened automatically after a tool result. | **[App](/agent/app-panel)** |
| **Creator** | **Discoverable apps** for the project you’re in; opening a **new creator** usually starts an **automatic trial** (see below). | **[Creator](/agent/creator-tab)** |
| **Directory** | Search **across creators** and open apps from the wider catalog. | **[Directory](/agent/directory)** |

## Ad-hoc vs persistent apps

| | **Persistent** | **Ad-hoc** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **What it means** | You **connected** the app to your agent in **Config** (toggle on). Its tools stay available until you turn it off. | You **opened** the app from a **link**, **Directory**, or similar **without** fully adding it to your normal “pool” first. |
| **In Config** | Each app row has a **switch** to connect or disconnect. | The row may show **ad-hoc** and **no** permanent connect switch until you add it through the usual flow (often via the **Creator** tab). |
| **In chat** | Tools from **connected** apps are available to the agent. | The session can still use the open app; once you fully connect with a **new creator**, a **trial subscription** is created **automatically** (see below). |

## Trial when you use a new creator

The **first time** you connect with apps from a **creator** you haven’t had a subscription with before, Agentsyx **creates a trial subscription automatically**, based on how that creator set up their **trial plan** in Agentsyx. You are **not** asked to pay up front for that initial trial. When the trial ends or you need more, **upgrades** follow **that creator’s** paid plans—see **[Subscriptions](/subscriptions)**.

**Tip:** For day-to-day use, **connect** apps in **[Config](/agent/config)** so tools stay available in **[Agent (chat)](/agent/chat)**. Use **[Directory](/agent/directory)** to find more apps.
