# Agent workspace

The **Agent** area is usually the **home** view after you open your organization on **agentsyx.com**. It brings together **chat**, access to **tools** from connected apps, **embedded app panels** (when an app provides one), and **app discovery**.

On **desktop**, tabs are grouped (some on the left, some on the right); on **small screens** they appear in a **single row**.

## Agent (chat)

The **Agent** tab is **chat** connected to **tools** from apps you’ve **connected** to this agent (see **Config**). When a tool opens an app’s visual panel, the UI often switches to the **App** tab so you can use it there.

## Config (agent and connections)

Under **Config**, you **shape the agent** and **connect apps** to it:

- **Which agent is active** — Switch agents or **add** another (for example personal vs team-scoped agents, when available).
- **Agent profile** — **Name**, **description**, and **system message** (instructions for how the agent should behave in chat).
- **Apps connected to this agent** — Apps you’re allowed to use. Each app has a **switch** to **connect** or **disconnect** so its tools are (or are not) available in chat. Expanding a row lists **tools**, **resources**, and **prompts** from the live app, with **refresh**.

Apps you opened only **for this session** via a link (see **Ad-hoc vs persistent**) may appear as **ad-hoc**: there’s no **permanent connect** switch until you add the app through your normal flow.

## App (embedded panel)

The **App** tab shows the **embedded interface** for the app in context (tool picker, panel, and actions the app provides). Messages about **subscriptions** or **free trials** can appear here when a plan is required — see **[Subscriptions](/subscriptions)**.

## Creator tab (on agentsyx.com — not the operator console)

The tab labeled **Creator** here is **only on the customer site**. It is **not** the same as the **[Agentsyx Creator](https://creator.agentsyx.com)** console, where your organization’s operators configure orgs, connectors, and **plans**.

This tab lists **discoverable apps** you can open in this org/project. Plan and subscription steps follow **what your organization configured**; see **[Subscriptions](/subscriptions)**.

## Directory (app directory)

The **Directory** tab is the **cross-organization app directory**: search **creators and apps**, browse grouped results, and **open** an app. That takes you to the right place and **App** experience — similar to opening a **deep link**.

## Ad-hoc vs persistent app use

| | **Persistent** | **Ad-hoc** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Meaning** | You **turned the app on** for your agent in **Config** — tools stay available until you turn it off. | You **opened** the app from a **link**, **Directory**, or similar **without** adding it to your long-term connected set first. |
| **In Config** | **Switch** to connect/disconnect. | Row labeled **ad-hoc**; no persist switch until you add it properly. |
| **Chat** | Tools from **connected** apps feed the agent. | The open app can still drive the session and **App** tab. |
| **Subscription** | Your organization’s usual rules. | **Paid** app with **no** subscription → **subscription required** and a path to **choose a plan** (often via the **Creator** tab). |

**Tip:** For **everyday use**, **connect** apps in **Config** so tools stay in **Agent** chat. Use **Directory** to **discover** other apps.
