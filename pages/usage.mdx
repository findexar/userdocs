# Usage

The **Usage** view (sidebar on **agentsyx.com**) shows how **credits** are **used** and how **tool activity** adds up over time for **you** in the current org/project.

## What you see

- **Credit balance and trends** — A high-level picture of consumption (charts or summaries, depending on product version).
- **Usage history** — A **history** of tool usage so you can see **what** drove spend (helpful for “why did my credits drop?”).

## If something looks wrong

- Open **Usage** when you’re wondering about **quota**, **overage**, or tools **stopping** after credits ran out.
- Also check **[Subscriptions](/subscriptions)** if the issue might be **plan tier** or **entitlement**, not raw consumption.

Limits and pricing are configured by your organization in **Creator**; this screen **reflects** what Agentsyx records for your account.
