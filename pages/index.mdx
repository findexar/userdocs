# Overview

On **[agentsyx.com](https://www.agentsyx.com)** you use **assistant apps** from **creators** (the teams behind those apps), manage **subscriptions and credits**, and work in an **agent** (chat, tools, and in-page app panels). **Plans and pricing** are set by **each creator** in Agentsyx; this site explains what **you** see and how to get around.

## Layout

- **Header** — Account and global navigation.
- **Sidebar** — Main navigation on larger screens; on phones and small tablets the same items appear in a **slide-out menu**.
- **Main area** — Either the **[Agent workspace](/agent)** (the usual “home” view) or a full-page view for **[Subscriptions](/subscriptions)**, **[Usage](/usage)**, or **API Key** (see below).

## Sidebar destinations

| Item | Purpose |
| ---- | ------- |
| **Agent** (or your agent’s name) | Opens the **[Agent workspace](/agent)** — chat, **Config**, **App**, **Creator**, **Directory**. |
| **My Subscriptions** / **My Subscription** | **[Subscriptions](/subscriptions)** — your **plans** and billing state. Labels may be singular or plural depending on your deployment. |
| **Usage** | **[Usage](/usage)** — credits, trends, and recent tool activity. |
| **API Key** | Create or rotate an **API key** when a creator’s app offers programmatic access through Agentsyx. |
| **Help** | Opens this help site in a new tab (when your deployment enables it). |

## In this section

- **[Agent workspace](/agent)** — Tabs (Agent, Config, App, Creator, Directory), **ad-hoc vs persistent** apps, app directory.
- **[Subscriptions](/subscriptions)** — Where subscription and plan flows appear for you.
- **[Usage](/usage)** — How you see credit consumption.
- **[B2B (future)](/b2b-future)** — Planned team and organization-level experiences.
